Setup

Do This Today

  1. Install Discord on the device you will using during training.
  2. Create an account with your username formatted as FirstName LastName SAP.
  3. Click the REGISTER NOW! button below on the device you'll use during training.
  4. Set your voice settings in Discord to Push To Talk.
  5. Customize your profile with a work appropriate photo.

Do This On {{Training Start Date}}

  1. At 9:30 AM, Open Discord on the device you will be using for training.
  2. Locate the training environment on your server list.
  3. Connect to the General voice channel.
  4. Ensure you are able to hear and speak using Push To Talk
  5. Be ready to begin at 10 AM!

Work with your home or training store Manager for support.

Other Details

  • Register ASAP after receiving the results of your background check to ensure your spot!

  • Each session begins promptly at 10 AM and you will need to be in the training environment 15 minutes prior to the training start time.

  • Attendance is mandatory for all 15 days.

  • You will need a set of headphones with a built-in microphone and an Android or iOS phone or tablet for using Discord. If you do not have these items, your store management may be able to loan the necessary items to you (for use in-store only).

  • Bring a charger and leave it at the store throughout the first week of training if possible! You will be using your device all day!

  • Wi-fi access is highly recommended throughout the three weeks of Ready! training as this course relies heavily on VoIP technology.

  • Indentify who your local technical support person will be throughout training. This will most likely be your store manager or training store manager, but you may be assigned a specific person to help you with technical issues.

  • Please do not utilize Discord for work communication outside of work hours!
