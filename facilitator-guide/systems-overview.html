Intro

Purpose

At the end of this module, Mobile Associates (MAs) will:

  • Have awareness of the T-Mobile systems available to support customer interactions.

Explain

  • At T-Mobile, there are multiple systems to support our customers.
  • Using the systems properly can increase customer satisfaction and drive an Un-carrier experience in the stores.
  • Systems are a big deal and require a lot of practice.
  • The three systems we are going to cover are:
    • Watson: Used to capture customer information to perform credit checks and activate new postpaid accounts.
    • Point of Sale (POS/mPOS): Used to collect payment for device and accessory purchases, bill payments, EIP payments, setup new installment plans, and accept trade-ins.
    • QuikView: Used to support existing customers’ accounts and perform account audits.

Ask

What are some examples of how these systems are used based on your sales floor activities observations?

Sample answers:

  • Watson: To add a line to an existing customers account.
  • Point of Sale (POS/mPOS): To pull up a receipt for a previous purchase.
  • QuikView: To find out if a customer was available for an upgrade.

Debrief

  • What are the benefits of Watson, POS/mPOS, and QuikView for the customer experience?
    • Watson: Provides a smooth transition for customers when porting in from a different carrier.
    • POS/mPOS: Ability to be flexible with a customer and move around the store by using mPOS.
    • QuikView: Provides customers with account details including rate plan and features, to eliminate account confusion.
  • Which Interaction Model behaviors could a Mobile Expert demonstrate while using these systems?
    • Make it Personal: Have a two-way conversation while reviewing a customer’s account in QuikView or activating service on Watson.
    • Guide the Purchase: Ask personalized questions to determine which services would benefit the customer based on their account in QuikView.
  • What actions can be performed with Watson, POS/mPOS and QuikView?
    • Watson: Add a line, new postpaid activation, business activations, etc.
    • POS/mPOS: Complete device and accessory transactions, process prepaid refills, and customer’s bill payments.
    • QuikView: Change customers rate plan and features.
