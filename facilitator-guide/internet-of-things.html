Intro

Purpose

Mobile Associates (MAs) will be given time to explore T-Mobile’s line-up of IoT devices, with emphasis on SyncUP DRIVE, Connected Home products, and FamilyMode.

After successfully completing this module, they will be able to:

  • Define Internet of Things.
  • Describe the benefits of SyncUP DRIVE.
  • Describe the benefits of the Nest Security Pack.
  • Describe the benefits of FamilyMode.
  • Identify Connected Accessories sold in their store.

Ask

Ask and discuss in your own words:

  • Who’s heard of Internet of Things (IoT)?
  • Who can give an example of an IoT product?
    • Sample answer: SyncUP DRIVE

Explain

  • IoT is huge. By some estimates there will be 100 billion “things” connected by 2020.
  • For example, Samsung’s CEO has said that by 2020, “every single piece of Samsung hardware will be an IoT device, whether it is an air purifier or an oven.”
  • While we might not sell ovens, we do have a wide selection of IoT products that can benefit customers, and that represent a whole new playing field of sales opportunities for you.
  • If you haven’t already experienced a shift in thinking, now is the time to realize we are so much more than a cell phone company.
  • Today, you’ll be learning about some of our IoT products with an emphasis on SyncUP DRIVE, Connected Home products, and FamilyMode.
  • You’ll notice SyncUP FLEET. It’s similar to SyncUP DRIVE, but is primarily for T-Mobile for Business customers who have a fleet of vehicles they want to track and manage.

DIY Activity

Purpose

Learn about IoT devices, the benefits of SyncUP DRIVE, Connected Home, and FamilyMode, and the rate plans that correspond to these products.

Instructions

TIME: 5 min setup, 1 hr research, 20 min debrief

Instruct learners to explore the range of IoT products, and specifically learn more about SyncUP DRIVE, Connected Home, and FamilyMode.

  1. If possible, visit the sales floor to see which IoT products are being sold.
  2. Learn about specific IoT products:
    1. SyncUP DRIVE Infomercial (428552)
    2. SyncUP DRIVE Overview (427912)
    3. Connected Home Hub (437248)
    4. FamilyMode Grab & Go (442073)
    5. FamilyMode Hub (442173) – choose two videos to watch
    6. Nest Secure WBT (Un-carrier Academy)
    7. FamilyMode WBT (Un-carrier Academy)
  3. Explore other IoT products that catch your eye: https://c2.t-mobile.com/community/sales-training/iot

Note: These WBTs are assigned to all new MAs, but appear in their main transcript, rather than within the Ready! curriculum.

Debrief

  • What are the key features of SyncUP DRIVE?
    • Sample answers: locate your car, Wi-Fi hotspot, diagnose problems
  • What benefits does the Nest Security Pack have over older alarm systems?
    • Sample answers: manage from your smartphone, video monitoring
    • What are three features of FamilyMode and how they can benefit customers?
    • Sample answer: location tracking to ensure your family is safe
    • What are some benefits of the FamilyMode Home Base accessory?
    • Sample answer: you can manage devices that are connected to your Wi-Fi router
  • What other IoT products did you discover?

Video Activity

Purpose

Practice explaining IoT products, their benefits, and pricing.

Instructions

TIME: 5 min setup, 30 min video, 25 min debrief

Bring IoT products into real life.

  • Think of a friend or family member who could benefit from SyncUP DRIVE or the Nest Security Pack, and decide the top three ways the product could benefit them.
  • Create a short video with your phone or a demo phone, including:
    • A simple explanation of the product.
    • The top three ways you believe the product will benefit them.
  • Send it to your friend, family member, or manager.
  • Explain the video is a training activity and ask for their feedback.
  • Remind MAs to keep their videos within our social media guidelines.
  • Assume the person is interested in this IoT product. Prepare a breakdown of pricing (device cost, EIP option, and rate plan cost).

Debrief

  1. Let’s hear about your friends and family videos.
    • Who did you choose?
    • Which product?
    • Have you received feedback yet on your video?
  2. Let’s have some volunteers re-enact their video.
