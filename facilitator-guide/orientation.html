About Us

KCI is a wireless retailer and wholesaler based in Houston, TX. The company was founded in 1993 (just had 25th anniversary), back when most people were still carrying pagers and used pay phones. Do any of you remember pagers?

We have been a partner of T-Mobile for over 17 years and as a partner of T-Mobile, we:

  • Have T-Mobile retail stores that we own and operate.
  • We also act as a wholesaler. T-Mobile refers to us as a Master Dealer
  • We're a retailer and wholesaler for Metro by T-Mobile.

So let’s talk a little more about what KCI does.

As a T-Mobile retailer, we own and operate stores that T-Mobile refers to as TPR. TPR stands for T-Mobile Premier Retailer. As TPR stores, we operate T-Mobile’s highest caliber branded retail locations.

These stores look & feel just like a T-Mobile owned store. In order to have stores in this program, we must follow very strict operating guidelines and achieve specific sales goals. T-Mobile regularly audits our store locations to make sure we are meeting their guidelines.

We own and operate retail stores in Texas, Oklahoma and Louisiana.

A surprising statistic is that around 70% of T-Mobile’s stores are owned and operated by a separate companies like KCI.

Although our stores are not franchises, the comparison is similar.

We also operate as a Master Dealer for T-Mobile. This means that there are other stores that sell T-Mobile products and services under KCI. We pay them commissions and provide training and support to these "Sub Dealers"
