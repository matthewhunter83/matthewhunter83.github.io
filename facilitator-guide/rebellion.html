Intro

Purpose

At the end of this module, Mobile Associates (MAs) will be able to:

  • Describe Rebellion and demonstrate how to navigate the system.
  • Apply system knowledge when working with customers.

Explain

  • Our focus has been on delivering a customer experience that sets us apart from the competition.
  • We introduced the Interaction Model that brought the Un-carrier Experience to life with our in-store behaviors.
  • But, our systems haven’t caught up, until now!
  • This is just the beginning! Rebellion sets us up to build the foundation for what we want the company to become.
  • Let’s check out a video to see how amazing the next step in our Un-carrier journey is!

Action

Play the Welcome to Universe 2.0 video.

Ask

  • What are some benefits of Universe 2.0 called out in the video?
    • Fewer screens, fewer clicks meaning customers receive resolution faster than ever.
    • Breaks down complexity and unifies our frontline teams.
  • How does Universe 2.0 benefit our customers?
    • Unified experience, customers see the same experience employees do.
    • Provides customers with the information they need to manage their own accounts.

Explain

  • The system is called Rebellion, used for prepaid activations and account maintenance.
  • Rebellion provides a simple way to drive an Un-carrier experience through any channel. We don’t do complicated.
  • The frontline, customer care, and our customers get the information they need when they need it and all see the same system!
  • Where possible everything should be done using REMO. If not, you can use a PC.

FAQ Activity

Explain

  • When using the Rebellion system, you may have some questions.
  • We developed a way to address the common questions you may have with a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs).

Instructions

TIMING: 5 min setup, 5 min activity, 10 min debrief

  1. Split the class into groups of 2-3.
  2. Provide learners with a printed copy or the link to the UFO – Here We Go! FAQ handout (439121)
  3. Assign one or more numbered questions from the UFO – Here We Go! FAQ handout.
    1. Example: "Terry or Group 1, your questions are number 5 and 13."
  4. Learners should search the UFO – Here We Go! FAQ document for that question and answer. Study the Q&A and then be prepared to share the Q&A, with the larger group.

Note: Questions 12 & 13 are browser specific. If your store has Citrix assign question 13. If your store does not use Citrix assign question 12.

Debrief

  • Ask for volunteers to read their question and answer out loud to the larger group.
  • Recommend learners to bookmark the UFO – Here We Go! FAQ C2 page for future reference.

DIY Activity

Explain

  • It’s time to see how Rebellion works.
  • The system WalkMe will walk you through navigating Rebellion step-by-step.
  • For questions during the DIY activity, use the UFO – Here We Go! FAQ’s C2 page. (439121)

Action

This activity works best if each learner has their own REMO or desktop. If REMOs or a desktop is not available for each MA, partner them up for the DIY activity.

  • Take turns and explore additional trainings of your choice!
  • Each person should use their own login to get credit for completing trainings.

Instructions

TIMING: 5 min setup, 2 hr WalkMe, 15 min debrief

  1. Access (this link) in Chrome on a computer or Safari on a REMO.
  2. Log in with your NT Login.
  3. Select Start in the pop-up window.
  4. Select the Training button in the lower right-hand corner to launch the main menu.
  5. Complete all six topics:
    • Getting Started
    • Account Refills
    • Account Maintenance
    • Activations
    • Devices
    • Resources
  6. During each WalkMe, have learners fill out the Getting Underway – Fill in the Blank activity sheet. (439174)

Note: When learners are reviewing the Resources C2 documents these are best accessed on the desktop.

Debrief

  • Review the Getting Underway – Fill in the Blank activity sheet with the learners.
  • Use the Getting Underway – Fill in the Blank activity sheet for leaders to find the answers to the activity sheet (439178).
  • Take time to review any answers that the learners struggled with.

Debrief

  • Rebellion is simple to use and navigate, eliminating excess wait time for customers.
  • When working with customers, Explore & Discover and help identify how Rebellion could benefit them.
