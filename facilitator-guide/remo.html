Intro

Purpose

At the end of this module, Mobile Associates (MAs) will be able to:

  • Use REMO as a side-by-side selling tool when interacting with customers.
  • Search, define, and describe how REMO is used during a customer interaction.

Explain

  • REMO is the ultimate side-by-side selling tool that provides an in-store Un-carrier experience.
  • REMO allows you to work side-by-side with your customers.
  • With REMO you are 100& mobile in the store, you can demonstrate products and services without having to shuffle between a desk and the sales floor.
  • This tool gives you the flexibility needed to review a customer’s account or run credit without stepping away from the customer, removing all barriers.

Ask

During sales floor activities, what ways have you seen REMO used with customers?

Sample answers:

  • Pull up a customer’s upgrade availability.
  • Run a customer’s credit.
  • Pull up a customer’s bill.

Debrief

  • How does utilizing REMO remove barriers during a customer interaction?
    • Promotes interactivity with your customer.
    • Builds trust.
    • Creates better relationships between MA and customer.
  • What information can you access from REMO to perform an effective account audit?
    • Credit class
    • Tenure
    • Billing amount
    • Past due balance
    • Lines available
  • Why is it important to be knowledgeable about the tools, resources, and transactions on REMO?
    • We want to process transactions with consistency across all stores.
    • Confidence builds customers’ trust.
