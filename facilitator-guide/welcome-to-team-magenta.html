Icebreaker: Body Activity

Purpose

  • Invite learners to uncover common interests, goals, or dreams.
  • Discover what learners are passionate about as a group.

Explain

We are going to spend the next three weeks together! Let’s complete an activity to get to know one another a bit more.

Instructions

Instruct learners to fill out each area of the body explaining a little bit about themselves.

  • Head: Dream or goal
  • Ears: Things we listen to
  • Eyes: How people see us
  • Hands: Things we make/do
  • Stomach: Favorite food
  • Heart: Strong value/belief
  • Right foot: Places we visit
  • Left foot: Place we wish to visit

Invite participants to share their personal responses one at a time.

Note: This activity is well suited for spreading out over multiple days. Depending on class size, consider having participants share one of their responses at the start of class each day.

Expectations Activity

Purpose

Prompt learners to create expectations for the class.

Action

Use either a note pod or short answer poll to collect and store class expectations and learner agreement.

Instructions

TIME: 10 min

  1. Ask Mobile Associates (MAs) what we need to do to have a great learning experience.
  2. Gather responses and add to the list.
  3. Ensure the list includes:
    • Own your own learning
    • Respect others
    • Maintain confidentiality
    • Participate and have fun
    • Silence devices
    • Arrive on time

Explain

Let’s use this list to create a learning agreement so we know the specific actions and behaviors we should abide by. For example:
  • Own your own learning by being here now. This means: staying engaged in the discussion and activities, avoiding side conversations, and focusing on active listening.
  • Respect others by acknowledging that we all come from different backgrounds and experiences and bring something unique to the table. Be respectful of the ideas shared by your teammates.
  • Maintain confidentiality by not sharing information outside of training that may hurt others. Training is a safe environment.
  • Participate and have fun in discussions, sharing your ideas, and engaging with activities and Skill Practices. Your participation is the key to your success. This is your opportunity to practice and learn before you hit the sales floor!
  • Silence devices to make sure you’re not battling distractions while you’re in training.
  • Arrive on time from breaks and lunch to make sure we are being respectful of each other’s time and staying on track.
These classroom expectations put everyone on the same page to start Ready! They will be posted throughout training so we can reference it as needed.

Action

After the expectations list is complete, place it in a visible location.

Career Path

Purpose

  • Give MAs a clear picture of their career path.
  • Show MAs that T-Mobile takes pride in developing employees.

Note: TPR may use this time to discuss their company’s career development opportunities.

Explain

  • Career development is very important at T-Mobile.
  • We have designed a clear path of success for your personal and professional growth. It starts right now!
  • Today is your first day as a Mobile Associate.
    • The Ready! program will provide initial training and certify your skills and knowledge.
  • After Ready! you will enter the Mobile Expert Certification program.
    • The ME Certification process builds on Ready!
    • You will have more in-depth training and hands-on practice. Once completing all of the ME Certification requirements, you become a Mobile Expert!

Explain

Let’s take a closer look at Ready! and Mobile Expert Certification.

About Ready!

  • Ready! is a consistent, best in class, three-week on-boarding program.
  • It focuses on three key areas, or themes for each week.
    • Week 1: Providing the Un-carrier Experience
    • Week 2: Products and Services Expertise
    • Week 3: Systems, Tools, and Resources Proficiency
  • Certification prepares you to enter your home store on the next step in your learning journey; to practice and perform on the sales floor as a Mobile Associate.
  • Certification requires a passing score of 85% or above.

Explain

About Mobile Expert Certification

  • The ME Certification program provides a multi-path strategy that allows you to transition from Ready! into your learning role within your home store.
  • Each path provides clear checkpoints and measures of success to drive progress and skill development.
  • Each path includes a closing checkpoint that allows MAs to show what they know.
  • After completing all four paths, MAs are ready to complete ME Certification, which requires a passing score of 85% or above.
  • MAs are promoted to an ME once Certification is achieved.
  • Becoming an ME involves a change in compensation and commission to go along with the new title.

Ask

What questions do you have about the Ready! program or Mobile Expert Certification at a high level?

Explain

Next we’ll go into more detail about the Ready! program, since this is how you will be spending your next three weeks.

Ins and Outs of Ready

Purpose

  • Provide a high-level overview of the Ready! program.
  • Explain the levels of detail in which topics are presented.
  • Review the Ready! Certification strategy.

Explain

This three-week instructor-led course provides you with the foundational knowledge on what is needed to be an MA and learn what it means to be Un-carrier.

  • You will learn how to deliver an unforgettable customer experience by developing expertise in three key areas.
    • Providing the Un-carrier Experience
    • Products and Services Expertise
    • Systems, Tools, and Resources Proficiency
  • This is a highly interactive course filled with a mix of:
    • Classroom training
    • Self-guided learning
    • Web-based trainings (WBTs)
    • Knowledge Checks
    • Skill Practices

Topics are covered at the following levels of detail:

  • Awareness: Define and describe the topic.
  • Overview: Search and find answers on your own.
  • Deep Dive: Try and apply what you have learned.

This allows for exploration, practice, and in-depth discovery. The goal is to ensure MAs remain aligned with the content, while working at their own pace.

Explain

Ready! Certification is comprised of the following.

Three Knowledge Checks (Days 5, 10, and 15)

  • Learners demonstrate knowledge of topics covered each week.
  • The passing threshold is 85% for each Knowledge Check.
  • Learners may use their resources (notes, C2, T-Nation, etc.).

Two Skill Practice Evaluations (Days 9 and 14)

  • Learners demonstrate mastery of the Interaction Model (438049).
  • Learners must earn a cumulative passing score of 85% to certify.

Pay Philosophy

Purpose

Explain KCI Wireless’ pay philosophy clearly, without mentioning dollar amounts.

Explain

There should not be a trade-off between selling and serving.

  • At KCI Wireless, when you provide great service and create an Un-carrier Experience, sales are a natural outcome of your efforts.
  • How you help Guide The Purchase is by Being Your Un-carrier Self, and inviting your customer to be themselves, as they Explore and Discover all that T-Mobile has to offer.
  • The powerful difference every MA creates for customers isn’t limited to pricing, plans, or even the latest devices. The difference is YOU.
  • When everyone takes the time to Make It Personal and Focus on the Customer, we all win together, and our customer wins too.
  • Whether you’re working to make it possible for your customer to say, “Problem Solved!” or walking through onboarding steps that help your customer Stay Connected, your words, actions, and customer focus has the largest impact on T-Mobile’s success, and yours.

Explain

  • As a Mobile Associate you’ll receive base pay.
  • This is a learning role, so there is no commission
  • During your onboarding journey, you will receive three milestone bonuses.
    1. After completing and passing Ready! Certification.
    2. When you complete Mobile Expert Certification.
    3. A final bonus after sales metrics are achieved.

Mohawk Activity

Purpose

Invite learners to creatively share who they are, what makes them unique, and reinforce the importance of expressing their authentic personality with a "no robots" approach to customer interaction.

Instructions

TIME: 30 min create, 20 min debrief

  1. Print and distribute the Mohawk Activity Template (437475) and art supplies.
  2. Visually represent yourself on the mohawk, such as:
    1. Your name
    2. What makes you unique
    3. What you love
    4. Your motivation, goals, accomplishments, or struggles
    5. What makes you proud
  3. As a facilitator, create your own mohawk and share.
  4. Once complete, invite each learner to share their design.

Note: Stores do not typically have art supplies, so creating the Mohawk on their device using an app like Instagram works well.

Note: Depending on class size, consider having participants share their mohawks over the course of several days.

Debrief

  • When has a person helping you in a store made the experience so amazing that you planned to ask for them by name the next time, or even recommended them to a friend or family member?
  • What about that person made you feel that way?
  • Why is personality such an important part of a great customer experience?

Explain

It’s all about the customer! This means being your Un-carrier self. Bring your authentic personality, excitement, curiosity, and expertise to make the experience memorable, meaningful, and truly world-class every day.
