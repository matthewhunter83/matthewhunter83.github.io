DIY Activity

Purpose

This activity will enable Mobile Associates to:

  • Explain how Layer3 TV works and what makes it unique.
  • Use the Interaction Model to sell Layer3 TV.

Instructions

TIME: 1 hour 45 min research, 30 min debrief.

Instruct Mobile Associates to:

  1. Access Un-carrier Academy, and self-assign the Layer3 TV curriculum.
  2. Complete each of the four sections of the Layer3 TV curriculum:
    • Layer3 TV Introduction and Terminology
    • Layer3 TV Packages, Programming, and Equipment
    • Layer3 TV Value and How It Works
    • Layer3 TV Sales Methodology and Process
  3. Take notes and jot down any questions they have.

Debrief

Here are some ideas to check for understanding. Feel free to add your own!

  1. What is “TV That Learns You?”
    • A personalized home screen.
    • Organized recommendations for each user.
    • Less time searching, more time watching!
  2. What is the difference between Over-the-Air, Over-the-Top, and Pay TV?
    • Over-the-Air: Locally broadcasted TV, picked up by an antenna.
    • Over-the-Top: Video streamed over the internet (e.g., Netflix, Hulu).
    • Pay TV: Video delivered via a private network (e.g., Xfinity, Dish).
  3. What are we referring to when we say SD, HD, or 4K?
    • Video resolution and quality.
  4. What’s the difference? SD | HD | 4K
    • SD, or Standard Definition, is the lowest quality of the three. The resolution is 480 pixels (480p).
    • HD, or High Definition, is in the mid-range quality of the three. The resolution is 720 pixels (720p) or 1080 pixels (1080p).
    • 4K, or Ultra High Definition, is the highest quality of the three. It has four times the number of pixels compared to HD.
  5. What is “Transparent Billing?”
    • Making billing simple.
    • No hidden fees! No HD fee, no DVR fee, etc.
    • Layer3 TV’s approach to transparent billing is different than T-Mobile ONE. Specifically, with T-Mobile ONE, taxes and fees are included, but while there are no hidden fees with Layer3 TV, taxes are not included.
  6. What is “TV everywhere?”
    • If a network has a mobile app, you can log into it with your Layer3 TV account to watch TV on-the-go.
  7. What are some behaviors from the Interaction Model that apply to selling Layer3 TV?
    • Ask personalized questions.
    • Have a two-way conversation.
    • Spend time on what matters.
    • Match customer needs.
    • Offer a competitive comparison.
    • Demonstrate products and features.
    • Give your personal recommendations.
    • Connect on a customer’s terms.
    • Overcome hesitations.
  8. What final questions do you have about Layer3 TV before we wrap up today?
