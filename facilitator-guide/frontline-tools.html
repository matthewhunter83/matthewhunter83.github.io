Intro

Purpose

At the end of this module, Mobile Associates (MAs) will be able to:

  • Use Retail Valet during a customer interaction.
  • Explain the benefits of using Retail Valet with every customer, every time.

Explain

  • Retail Valet is another way the Un-carrier eliminates pain points for customers.
  • Retail Valet is best used on a REMO but can be accessed on a desktop computer.
  • This tool helps stores manage the customer queue and wait times.
  • MAs can set expectations with customers on approximately how long before they’ll be waited on.
  • On T-Mobile.com, customers can add themselves to the line using Get in Line and get approximate wait times.
  • For accurate wait times, every customer should be entered into the queue.
  • By adding each customer and selecting their reason for their visit, Retail Valet will provide valuable insight for store managers.
  • These insights on why customers visit the store help managers coach their staff for better results.

Explain

  • We want to make it as easy as possible for customers. We don’t do complicated!
  • Shows customers we value their time.
  • Sets realistic expectations around how much time they may have to wait before being served.
  • Retail Valet gives customers more freedom of movement. For example, they can step out for coffee while waiting.

DIY Activity

Instructions

TIMING: 5 min setup, 20 min DIY activity, 5 min debrief

  1. Research on C2:
    • Retail Valet (419631)
    • Retail Valet Training and FAQ (431769)
  2. Obtain hands on practice using the Retail Valet test account.
  3. Navigate to Retail Valet on REMO or desktop through a web browser at https://t-mobile.retailvalet.com/Login/
  4. Use the following credentials to sign in to Retail Valet:
    • NTID: 02test (substitute the “02” in 02test with any number up to 15 when multiple users are using training accounts)
    • Password: magenta
    • Store Number: 3
  5. Add a sample customer, ex. Test-John Legere.
  6. Select a reason for the visit.
  7. Save to Queue.
  8. Select Serve Now.
  9. Select Finish Serving.

Note: There are 14 test accounts to be shared during training. (02test – 15test) Assign a different test account to each participant.

Debrief

  • What are some benefits of using Retail Valet?
    • Customers are given the freedom to spend their wait time however they want.
    • Removes the guessing out of who is next in line to be helped.
  • If the store isn’t busy, can we skip using Retail Valet and just help the customer?
    • For accurate wait times and reporting, every customer should be entered into the queue.
    • Retail Valet calculates wait times based on transaction types and the number of associates logged into the system.
  • What are the benefits of everyone working in the store being logged in to Retail Valet?
    • Customers will receive accurate wait times.
    • Customers will be helped in order if everyone working is logged in during their shift.
