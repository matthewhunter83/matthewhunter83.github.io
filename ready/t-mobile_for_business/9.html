Leads

  • A lead is someone who can lead you to a T-Mobile for Business sale based on owning a business or knowing someone who owns a small business.
  • There are three types of leads in Retail:
    • Walk-in Lead – business owner or decision maker who comes into your store interested in doing business with T-Mobile.
    • Referral Lead – someone who knows a small business owner and is willing to give you their contact information.
    • Store Prospecting Lead – a lead discovered through proactively contacting nearby businesses, leveraging existing relationships, using business lists, etc.
